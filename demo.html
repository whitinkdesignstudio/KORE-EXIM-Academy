How the Program Works?

Our program is designed to take participants through the complete export journey from understanding global markets to executing real export transactions, with an execution first approach.

🌏
Market & Product DiscoveryUnderstand how to evaluate product demand, which countries import specific products, how exporters select profitable markets
🤝
Buyer Identification & OutreachLearn buyer finding techniques, export enquiry management, communication with global buyers
🚢
Export Execution & LogisticsGain insights into export documentation, shipping processes, logistics planning, international trade compliance
Learning Area What You Will Gain
1Program Duration 36 Days Program • 72 Hours of practical training covering the complete export journey from market research to shipment execution.
2Industry-Led Learning Sessions with active trade professionals including Custom House Agents (CHA), Shipping Line Executives, Supply Chain Experts, Chartered Accountants, and Export Promotion Council representatives.
3Export Documentation & Compliance Practical understanding of 70+ export documents including commercial invoice, packing list, bill of lading, certificate of origin, shipping bill, and customs clearance procedures.
4Product Selection & Market Opportunity Learn how exporters evaluate global demand, market potential, and export feasibility to identify profitable export products and target markets.
5Buyer Discovery (Execution-First Approach) Learn how to identify and approach international buyers first, validate demand, and structure supply accordingly using trade platforms, databases, and professional outreach strategies.
6Global Digital Marketing & Brand Building Learn how to generate international leads using LinkedIn outreach, B2B marketplaces (Alibaba, Global Sources), digital branding, and online export marketing strategies.
7Real Export Case Studies Analysis of real export scenarios including buyer sourcing, pricing strategy, negotiation, documentation, and shipment execution.
